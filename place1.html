Batu Ferringhi Penang

Batu Ferringhi is a popular beach destination located on the northern coast of Penang Island in Malaysia.

Known for its beautiful sandy beaches and clear waters, Batu Ferringhi attracts both locals and tourists seeking a tropical getaway.

Overall, Batu Ferringhi combines natural beauty with a lively atmosphere, making it a must-visit spot in Penang.

Tanjong Bungah Penang

Tanjong Bungah is a coastal suburb located between George Town and Batu Ferringhi on the northern part of Penang Island in Malaysia.

George Town Penang

The city is bustling with markets, such as Chowrasta Market and Little India, where visitors can shop for local produce, spices, textiles and souvenirs.

George Town is renowned for its vibrant street art scene. Murals and steel rod sculptures are scattered throughout the city, depicting everyday life and historical scenes, turning the streets into an open-air gallery.
