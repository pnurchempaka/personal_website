Tioman Island is a small, tropical island located off the east coast of Peninsular Malaysia in

the South China Sea. Known for its stunning natural beauty, the island features lush

rainforests, crystal-clear waters, and white sandy beaches.

It is a popular destination for tourists seeking activities such as snorkeling, diving, and jungle

trekking.

Tioman Island is also rich in marine life and coral reefs, making it a favored spot for

underwater enthusiasts.

Pulau Tioman is a 2 hour boat ride from Mersing Jetty on the south eastern coast of Malaysia.

This is the photo that I captured from the public boat that we took to get to the island.

The island maintains a laid-back atmosphere with several small villages and resorts, offering

a serene escape from bustling city life. Additionally, Tioman is part of the Mersing Marine

Park, which helps preserve its diverse ecosystem.
